Love is all about the Bonds of People.
Inmost Love is about the Bond I have to Myself.

I Love Myself and my own Heart:

I care for myself.
I begin with my body.
I begin with my time to breathe.
What I eat makes me, so I eat the goods of Earth.
How I move shapes me, so I move with Purpose.

I know myself.
I know what I can change.
I know what I cannot and what I need not.

I am my own friend.
I speak well to myself. I use kind words.
I make chances to trust myself.
Starting small and growing from there.
I believe in myself.
And when it’s time for others, I set my boundaries.

I accept myself.
I accept my appearance and my past.
I move forward in life by comparing myself only to who I was.

I control myself.
I have standards and live up to them.
I can make mistakes, so I do forgive myself.
I have today, so I have a chance, I have hope.

I am worthy of Goodness.
Despite any wrongs I have done.
Despite any wrongs that have been done to me.
I am always worthy of Goodness.
