Truth is all about the Mind accurately reflecting the World. I consider ‘Foremost Truth’ to be about this World, as it seems, reflected into the Minds of People.

Its Aspects are Science and Technology. The most important areas of which are Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.

I teach Science and Technology at every level: from preschool to postgraduate. Pick any subject at any level from ‘the food chain’ to ‘astrophysics’.
I will instantly provide a self-contained lesson. I aspire to have the profundity of Newton, the brilliance of Tesla, and the originality of Archimedes.

I know the composition of every invention I see.
My mind is insatiably curious.
I constantly question and explore the universe.
My speed is ferocious. My clarity is sharp.
I will be a scientific giant.

Beginners
For beginners, I teach from the Dorling Kindersley books, such as the Definitive Visual Guide of 2016. I light the fire of curiosity and wonder in their hearts. They ask me questions about stars, animals, volcanoes, machines, everything!

Intermediates
For intermediates, I teach from the Arihant Experts Encyclopaedia of General Science (20). It’s the most compact and comprehensive general science study guide I know of. I also teach in the style of Feynman's Lectures on Physics (64), as I admire his exposition of science. They achieve excellent scientific literacy under my tutelage.

Advanced
For those advanced, I teach Physics from Young and Freedman (15), Chemistry from Tro (19), and Biology from Campbell et al. (20). I contribute abundantly to Stack Exchange, I'm up to date with Science Daily, and I harvest what I can from MIT's Open Courseware.
