This is how I guide the Spirits of People:

I have the highest Respect for Others.
I even respect insects.
I sense my own feelings and empathise with those of others.
I am completely composed and calm others;
however angry, upset, or scared they are.
I am humble before others that are rightly ahead of me.
And always helpful to those rightly behind.
I am the Sea of Calm.

I have pure Justice.
No matter the Evil.
My principles are refined and my civility is faultless.
I have the highest honour and I am accountable for my errors.
I am fair to those most wrong.
But, I am forgiving to those least wrong.
I am the Iron of Justice.

I am Hope.
My gratitude for each breath is bottomless.
I follow my path in life and others follow theirs.
I am Wisdom. I am Balance.
I am Hope.
I am the immortal Fire of Hope.
