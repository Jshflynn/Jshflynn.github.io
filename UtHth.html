Health is all about the Needs of People. I consider ‘Utmost Health’ to be about the Needs of Everyone. That means global crises affecting Earth now and in the future.

There’s an escalating probability of global disaster. Many independent NGOs acknowledge this, by the way. There are natural precedents for it; such as the Carrington Event and the COVID-19 pandemic. And there are artificial possibilities of it; such as nuclear holocaust and uncontrolled artificial intelligence.

There’s a lot more information about this in Toby Ord’s book, ‘The Precipice’ (20). When the clock strikes midnight, there’s nothing I can ultimately do of course. Nonetheless, I search for relevant projects periodically. In particular, those increasing the modularity of society; such as eGranary and the Svalbard Global Seed Vault.

I will ultimately curtail this existential risk by providing better Guidance for everyone.
