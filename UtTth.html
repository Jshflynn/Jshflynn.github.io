Truth is all about the Mind accurately reflecting the World.
I consider ‘Utmost Truth’ to be the Mind able to reflect all Worlds.

With few resources, the most relevant art is Linguistics.
I believe without a sufficient understanding of language itself, I will have limitations for attaining Utmost Truth.
I'm currently deep in research in this area.
I'm reading Radford's Introduction (09) and Aronoff et al.'s Handbook.

With modern resources, the most relevant technology is Artificial Intelligence.
I'm currently deep in research in this area, too.

I’m using Russell and Norvig's (16) AIAMA and Goodfellow et al.'s Deep Learning books to get an academic overview.

For the mathematics side of things, I’m using Lang's 'Linear Algebra' (10) and Wasserman's 'All of Statistics' (04).

I’m using Blackburn's Learn PROLOG Now! and Severance's Python for Everybody.
These let me get on top of Prolog and Python for symbolic and connectionist approaches.

It's difficult to get non-sensationalist news, so I use preprints and journals and understand what I can.
The best to me is ArXiv and Nature Machine Intelligence.
