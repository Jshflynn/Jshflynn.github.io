Imagery
Using Gardner's Art Through the Ages, I've acquired an appreciation for the evolution of visual arts over time. I create digital art, at least once a week, using InkScape and distribute it on PixelFed.

Architecture
I've been connecting to architecture using Le Corbusier (95) and Vitruvius' Ten Books, as well as taking photos of great buildings where I travel to. I've been creating a tiny house plan with FreeCAD.

Cooking
I'm able to cook a vast array of vegan foods that my friends always ask for more of:
Chinese (v.) luosifen, American apple pie, Indian khichdi, Brazilian (v.) feijoada, Russian shchi, Indonesian (v.) nasi goreng, Mexican (v.) taco, Canadian poutine, Argentinian (v.) empanadas, Australian pavlova, Turkish baklava, Egyptian ful medames, French crêpes, Saudi Arabian (v.) kabsa, Japanese (v.) ramen, South African (v.) bobotie, Pakistani gulab jamun, German sauerkraut, Colombian (v.) Ajiaco, Spanish omelettes, Thai (v.) Pad Thai, Algerian couscous, Italian pizza, Israeli falafel.
