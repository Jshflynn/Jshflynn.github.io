Beauty is all about the World reflecting the Soul.
I consider ‘Inmost Beauty’ to be about reflecting itself.
In other words, dream.
For this, I use Music.

I've been using Miller and Shahriari's World Music: A Global Journey (12) book to explore the world's music. I enjoy a lot of folk music, like Korean, Algerian, and Argentinian. Whenever I travel, I look for local live music and instruments. Sometimes I deliberately sample music as anonymously as possible to get in touch with the sound. I use Clark et al.'s (15) Understanding Music to understand the theory of music. I can play the piano and enjoy Rachmaninoff and De Bussy, but especially improvising. I use LMMS and Audacity to make music, and FunkWhale to share music.
