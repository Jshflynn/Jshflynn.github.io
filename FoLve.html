Love is all about the Bonds of People.
Foremost Love is about the Bonds I have to those Dear to Me.
My family, my friends, children, and more.

To connect at all, I learn English deeper.
As this is the language of my Heart.
For every word I meet, I know how to say it.
I know how to rhyme it.
And I know where it comes from.
I know its weight.
I know its derivations
And especially its meanings.
For this, I use:
Vocabulary.com, OneLook
Etymonline, OED
I know Roget's Thesaurus inside out:
Peter Mark Roget (1852), Roget's Thesaurus of English Words and Phrases.
I personally use Seneker's Frontier Experience.
I also use Levenshtein's Dictionary.
