Beauty is all about the World reflecting the Soul.
I consider ‘Utmost Beauty’ to be about the World able to reflect All Souls.

With few resources, the most relevant Area is Literature. I enjoy reading Dickens, Tolkien, Shakespeare in English; Tolstoy in Russian; Don Quixote in Spanish; and many others. With modern resources, I create games. I enjoy abstract games like chess. I have a 2000 ELO rating and I can play it mentally. I'm currently using Godot and Blender to create free games for the OSGL. Richard Bartle's (03) Designing Virtual Worlds book enables me to understand them better.
